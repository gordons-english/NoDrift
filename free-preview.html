NoDrift V1 English Codex Edition

A control system that teaches AI to follow your rules, check its work, and remember what matters.

Built to systematically reduce hallucinations, drift, blind agreement, stale answers, and lost context.

What This Is

NoDrift is a governance and memory kit for serious Codex work. It packages the workflow behind a governed documented Codex workspace work. The current internal record shows 0 confirmed drift, 0 confirmed hallucination, 1 candidate incident under review, and incomplete audit coverage.

The kit is not trying to control the AI model. It gives the workspace clear rules for what to trust, what to verify, what to record, and when to stop before action.

What It Helps With

  • Hallucinations and unsupported claims.
  • Drift across long conversations.
  • Blind agreement with the user.
  • Stale or unverified information.
  • Lost context after long work sessions.
  • Important topics and decisions buried inside long conversations.
  • Unapproved edits, uploads, commits, or publishing.

What Is Inside

  • Governance templates.
  • Project memory templates.
  • Approval and verification rules.
  • Context continuity protection.
  • Living Topic Maps and automatic topic branches.
  • Model routing guidance.
  • Setup prompts and checklists.

Why It Matters

The value is output density. When the workspace has rules, source boundaries, approval gates, and durable memory, and Living Topic Maps, less time is spent correcting drift, re-explaining decisions, searching for approved wording, cleaning up unsupported claims, or recovering lost context.
